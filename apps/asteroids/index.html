Asteroid Survival

Use the arrow keys to turn your ship.

Press the space bar to shoot bullets.

Rack up points by hitting asteroids, but dont let an asteroid collide with your ship!

  • To begin the game: 'enter'
  • To toggle mute: 'm'
  • To toggle pause: 'p'
